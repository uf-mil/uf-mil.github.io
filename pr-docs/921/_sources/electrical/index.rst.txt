Electrical
=====================
Welcome to documentation regarding the electrical team of MIL! Our team is responsible
for the construction and maintenance of the electrical systems on the lab's robots.
This includes developing firmware, circuitry, and embedded systems in order to allow
the bots to function at their highest capacity.

To get started, checkout the onboarding page!
